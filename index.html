Welcome

At Sunrise Ridge, located in the Southern Black Hills of South Dakota, our Golden Retriever puppies grow up with children in the house and are carefully trained to be well-behaved, friendly, and loving companions. Our focus is on producing puppies ready for family life who thrive in a nurturing, attentive environment.

About Us

We are a small, family-run breeder in the Southern Black Hills of South Dakota. With years of experience as a veterinary technician, we are passionate about raising healthy, happy Golden Retrievers who become cherished family members. Our dogs enjoy playful activities and close family interaction. We strive to improve the breed through careful parent selection and raising puppies in a loving, social, and well-trained environment.
